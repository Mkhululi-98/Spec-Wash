About Us

SPEC WASH provides professional cleaning services throughout Gauteng. We specialize in a variety of cleaning solutions, ensuring top-notch service and customer satisfaction.

Automotive Wash

We offer a thorough and professional cleaning for all types of vehicles. Our services include exterior washes, interior vacuuming, window cleaning, tire polishing, and waxing for a long-lasting shine. Whether it's a car, truck, or motorcycle, we ensure your vehicle looks and feels brand new!

Pavement & Roof Cleaning

Restore the shine of your driveways and roofs with our specialized cleaning techniques. We use high-pressure washing equipment and eco-friendly solutions to remove stains, moss, and dirt buildup, making your pavements and roofs look immaculate and well-maintained.

Wall Cleaning

We clean and brighten all types of walls, removing dirt, grime, and weather stains. Our team uses the best cleaning agents and techniques, including pressure washing and scrubbing, to restore your walls to their original beauty, whether for indoor or outdoor surfaces.

Fleet Truck Washing

Our fleet truck washing service keeps your commercial vehicles clean and professional-looking. We offer flexible cleaning schedules, including regular washes, detailing, and interior cleaning, ensuring your fleet is always in top condition for your business needs.

Fences & Gutter Clogs

We clear out clogged gutters and clean fences to maintain your property. Our team removes debris and buildup from gutters to prevent water damage, and we provide thorough fence cleaning to restore the look and integrity of wood, vinyl, or metal fences.

Floors

Our floor cleaning service ensures a spotless finish for your home or office. From tile and grout cleaning to hardwood floor polishing, we use industry-leading equipment and eco-friendly cleaners to make your floors shine and last longer. We tailor our approach to each floor type for the best results.
